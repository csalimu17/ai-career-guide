# **App Name**: AI Career Guide

## Core Features:

- Secure User Authentication & Onboarding: User signup and login via email/password or Google OAuth, with user profiles stored securely. Includes a modern onboarding flow to collect career goals and experience levels, then generating a personalized dashboard.
- CV Upload, Parsing & Structured Data Conversion: Allows users to upload existing CVs (PDF/DOC) and uses a third-party parsing tool to convert content into a structured, editable format for AI optimization.
- AI-Assisted CV Creation & Editor: A Canva-style drag-and-drop editor for building new CVs from scratch or editing parsed ones. Includes AI tools to generate content, rewrite bullet points with impact, suggest missing skills, and generate professional summaries.
- ATS Optimization & Scoring Tool: Analyzes CVs against specific job descriptions to provide an ATS compatibility score (0–100). This tool highlights missing keywords, formatting issues, and weak bullet points, offering actionable suggestions for improvement.
- Job Application Tracker: Enables users to add job applications, track their status (e.g., applied, interview, offer), attach specific CV versions used, and add private notes.
- Subscription Management & Template System: Integration with Stripe for managing 'Free', 'Pro', and 'Master' subscription plans. Provides ATS-friendly templates with a JSON-based structure, allowing users to easily switch designs and export as PDF.
- AI Assistant Chat: An interactive chat interface providing real-time AI guidance and advice on CV improvements, career suggestions, and general job search strategies as a powerful tool for users.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: a deep purple (#673AB7) for headings, active elements, and branding, symbolizing creativity and professionalism.
- Secondary color: a vibrant blue (#2196F3) used for interactive elements, links, and important highlights, representing reliability and trust.
- Accent color: a bright orange (#FF9800) to draw attention to call-to-actions, alerts, and key notifications, adding energy and visibility.
- Background color: A light grey (#F5F5F5) for a clean and unobtrusive canvas, ensuring readability and focus.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a modern grotesque-style sans-serif, chosen for its objectivity, versatility, and readability across various text sizes and content types.
- Clean, line-art or outline-style icons to maintain a modern, uncluttered aesthetic that complements the professional tone of the application.
- Responsive and intuitive layout design, optimizing for a Canva-style drag-and-drop editor with ample white space for content clarity, suitable for desktop and mobile.
- Subtle, smooth animations for transitions, hover states, and content updates to enhance the user experience and convey a premium feel without distraction.
